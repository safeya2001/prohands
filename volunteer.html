Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time commitment for volunteers?

Volunteering with Pro Hands is flexible. Most roles require 4–8 hours per week, depending on the project and your availability. We work around your schedule to ensure a comfortable experience.

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is required! We provide all necessary training and support. What matters most is your passion for the environment and willingness to learn and contribute.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! Our social media content creator role can be done entirely remotely. Other roles may require in-person attendance for events and workshops, but we also offer hybrid options.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, all active volunteers receive a certificate of participation upon completion of their commitment. We also provide recommendation letters for outstanding contributors.

Where are volunteer activities held?

Most activities take place in Amman, Jordan — at schools, community centers, and public venues. We occasionally organize events in other cities based on project needs.
