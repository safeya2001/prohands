## Content Summary

### Mission
Empowering communities through education, creativity, and environmental awareness for a sustainable future.

### Vision
We envision a sustainable Jordan where communities lead by example, inspiring environmental stewardship and responsibility through creative education.

### Values
1. **Inclusivity** - Ensuring equal access to programs for all genders, age groups, and communities
2. **Sustainability** - Advocating for long-term, impactful solutions to environmental challenges
3. **Creativity** - Leveraging art, performance, and social media to communicate environmental issues
4. **Community Engagement** - Involving local communities through collaborative workshops, performances, and projects
