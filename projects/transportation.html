Upcoming Project: Alternative Transportation Solutions

Promoting eco-friendly transportation options to reduce Amman's carbon footprint.

October 28, 2024 3 min read No Comments

Launching in 2024, Alternative Transportation Solutions will focus on promoting eco-friendly transportation options in Amman. The project will advocate for cycling, walking, and the use of public transport to reduce the city's carbon footprint.

Key Activities

  • Public Transport Maps — We will develop user-friendly maps to help residents navigate public transportation options more efficiently.
  • Cycling & Walking Initiatives — Training workshops will be held to promote cycling as a safe and healthy alternative to driving. Guided cycling tours will also be organized to explore nature while advocating for more bike-friendly infrastructure.
  • Community Advocacy — We'll work with local government to advocate for electric vehicles and public policies that support sustainable transport.
